How to Play Rodha

Rodha is a fast-paced platform game that challenges your timing, reflexes, and focus. Use the arrow keys or A and D to jump, double jump, and guide your character through a maze of moving traps, collapsing platforms, and narrow gaps. Each level introduces new hazards that test your precision, but clever players can learn the rhythm and reach the portal that marks the finish. Collect stars to unlock new skins and switch up the game’s visual themes. With 60 challenging skill levels, Rodha rewards patience, practice, and perfect jumps.
